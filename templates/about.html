About Shristi Beauty Store

Your premier destination for quality makeup products

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Shristi Beauty Store was born from a passion for high-quality, accessible makeup products. Our founder, a professional makeup artist, was frustrated with the lack of affordable yet effective products in the market.

What started as a small online shop has now grown into a thriving community of beauty enthusiasts, makeup artists, and customers who share our vision of making beauty accessible to everyone.

At Shristi Beauty, we believe that everyone deserves to feel confident and beautiful in their own skin. That's why we offer a carefully curated selection of products that cater to diverse skin tones, types, and preferences.

Our Values

Quality

We prioritize quality in every product we offer. Each item is carefully selected to ensure it meets our high standards.

Inclusivity

We believe in beauty for everyone. Our products cater to diverse skin tones and types, ensuring that everyone can find something that works for them.

Cruelty-Free

We are committed to ethical practices. All our products are cruelty-free, and we continue to expand our range of vegan options.
